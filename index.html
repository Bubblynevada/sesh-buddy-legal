Privacy Policy for Sesh Buddy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Sesh Buddy. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience, we may collect the following types of information:

Location Data

Sesh Buddy requests access to your device's location to:

  • Show you nearby spots on the map.
  • Allow you to add new spots at your current location.

This data is used solely for these core functionalities. You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device settings.

Photos and Camera

We request access to your device's camera and photo gallery only when you choose to:

  • Take a photo of a new spot.
  • Upload an existing photo to a spot listing.

We do not access your photos or camera without your explicit permission.

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect basic information such as your email address and username for authentication purposes and to manage your user profile.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our App's functionality.
  • Facilitate the creation and finding of spots.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect and defend our rights and property.

Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Sesh Buddy is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

User Choices & Controls

You have control over your data:

  • Location & Photos: You can manage permissions in your device settings.
  • Account: You may contact us to request deletion of your account and associated data.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at:

support@seshbuddy.app
