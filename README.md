Privacy Policy — Sesh Buddy

Last updated: 31/01/2026

Sesh Buddy respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Sesh Buddy mobile application.

Information We Collect

Sesh Buddy may collect the following information to provide core app functionality:

Location Data

We collect approximate or precise location data to:

Show nearby spots

Allow users to add new spots at their current location

Location data is only used while the app is in use.

Photos

If you choose to add photos to a spot, the app accesses your device’s camera or photo library.

Photos are uploaded only when you explicitly select them.

Account Information

Basic account details such as email address are used for authentication and profile features.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely to:

Display nearby locations and spots

Allow users to create and view spots

Show spot details on the map and in lists

Improve app reliability and user experience

We do not sell user data.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties for advertising.

Uploaded spot data (including photos and location) may be visible to other users of the app.

Data Storage & Security

Data is securely stored using industry-standard services.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect user data from unauthorized access.

Children’s Privacy

Sesh Buddy is not directed at children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can:

Disable location access via your device settings

Choose whether or not to upload photos

Request account or data removal by contacting us
